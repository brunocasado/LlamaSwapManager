# Code of Conduct

LlamaDeck is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

Be constructive, assume good intent, and focus criticism on ideas and code rather than people. Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Reports will be reviewed as confidentially as reasonably possible.
